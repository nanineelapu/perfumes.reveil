How to activate Msg91:
Add these keys to your .env.local once you receive them from the client:

env
MSG91_AUTH_KEY=your_msg91_auth_key
MSG91_FLOW_ID_CONFIRMED=your_flow_id_for_confirmation
MSG91_FLOW_ID_DELIVERED=your_flow_id_for_delivery
